About us

Our Humble Beginnings .

The carmelites Youth choir is a catholic church choir filled with young vibrant enthusiatic and commited christian youths that have a burning desire to serve there creater though singing. With there singing they aim to bring the lords people peace, joy, happiness and get them closer to there maker.

The choir was started in 2016 with a very small number of members but with more than sixty members now including foreigners from countries such as: congo, rwanda, burundi, kenya and many others.

Our Services

The functions we perform

  • In Mass

    We animate mass at kansanga parish church every sunday at 5:00pm in the evening. You are invited to give thanks to the lord

  • At Weddings

    The Carmelites youth choir also performs greatly and all weddings anywhere

  • On BirthDays or Graduations

    If you have a birthday and you plan to have mass, We are the choir for you.

    "Even without mass, you may call us and we party :-)"

  • At Funerals

    We animate funeral masses to help you bid fair-well to your loved ones

  • Public Address System

    The carmelites youth choir also owns a public address system that the are ready to rent out to you to aplify your function

  • All in All

    We perform at any functions you may have the involve mass animation or singing to praise the lord
